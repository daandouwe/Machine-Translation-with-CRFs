Introducing Commenting & Track Changes

Commenting and Track Changes will make it easier for you to work with peers in your documents.

Commenting

Want to discuss specific parts of the text?

Use our brand-new commenting system.

Track Changes

See changes in your documents, live.

Track, accept and reject changes individually.

Commenting

Just select a span of text and click on “Add comment”.

Comments can be replied to, resolved and permanently deleted.

Track Changes

Let your peers know what you've been up to.

Click on the “Track Changes” toggle to start marking your insertions, as well as your deletions.

Track Changes

Upon reviewing, changes can be accepted or undone.

Click “Accept” or “Reject” to incorporate or discard an individual change.
